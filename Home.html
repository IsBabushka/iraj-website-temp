Our Craft

Discover our range of specialty coffees—from classic Americanos and silky lattes to sweet caramel mochas—all crafted with carefully selected beans. Craving something cooler? Enjoy our iced blends, creamy frappes, or fruit-forward smoothies.

Brewing Mastery

  • Single-origin beans from sustainable farms
  • Small-batch roasting for optimal flavor
  • Three distinct brew methods available daily

Culinary Pairings

  • Freshly baked waffles with seasonal toppings
  • Artisanal cheese boards with local preserves
  • Signature spiced chicken wings (6 heat levels)

The Experience

Step into our rustic-industrial sanctuary featuring curated comfort and cutting-edge functionality. Our multi-sensory environment blends aromatic coffee notes with tactile materials and ambient acoustics.

Design Features

  • Reclaimed wood surfaces with ironwork detailing

  • Living wall with hanging plants & Freedom Walls to express your emotions and thoughts anonymously

Tech Integration

About Us

Nita's Coffee opened and started running on August, 2019 during the pandemic. Started in a mini shop/store in a town house village, then expanded and relocated during 2024. A unique coffee shop with both reggae and cozy vibes. Their quality coffee drinks makes their shop distinct with others. Soup dishes are also an option but only for those who like soups. Having a band playing while enjoying your coffee and snacks are truly a masterpiece of their coffee shop, which tend to attract more couples and students to spend their leisure time inside the coffee shop.
